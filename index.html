More About Me

Additionally, I have a certain degree of awareness of my own emotions; thus I tend to notice when I’m feeling a particular way and take my feelings into account when making decisions. I tend to think in both abstract ways—in terms of metaphors—and logically. Therefore, in my work and free time, I enjoy activities that get my “creative juices” flowing, but are also able to keep my mind on the primary objective of the task at hand.

My tendency to be both open-minded, yet realistic is generally quite advantageous. For instance, when there are no clear rules about how to approach a particular problem, my openness makes it easy for me to identify new ways to solve problems that might not be very obvious to people that are not as open as I am. However, because I’m also realistic-minded, I’m able to follow-rules quite easily. This combination makes it easy for me to excel in your work because I’m able to “think outside the box” while still being able to keep my eyes on “the big picture.” I’m someone who is moderate in openness, I have an appreciation for art and nature, but is also down to earth and realistic. I’m fond of music and art. But I also enjoy and appreciate things that have a clear point and some sort of practical utility.
